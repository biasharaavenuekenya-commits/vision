What You Get Inside VISION 2026

This is a premium, guided experience combining strategy, soul and accountability.

A 2025 Deep-Dive Audit

A guided debrief using three powerful lenses: 1. What worked and needs more of you, 2. What didn't and how you'll pivot, 3. What is staying in 2025 for good.

A Values-Led 2026 Vision Map

We translate what truly matters to you—your children, your health, your relationships, your business—into a vivid, specific picture of 2026 you can feel in your body.

A Practical 90-Day Execution Blueprint

Prioritised goals (life, family, business), Key offers or focus areas, Simple daily/weekly rhythms that support you instead of suffocating you.

Aligned Boundaries & Non-Negotiables

We define what you will no longer tolerate: in your calendar, your client relationships, your money, and your self-talk. These become your guardrails for the year.

Live Coaching & Hot Seat Support

This is not a passive webinar. You get direct coaching on your plan, your blind spots and your resistance, so you're not left alone staring at a pretty PDF.

Who This Is For

VISION 2026 is for you if:

  • You lead, coach, or run a business—and people depend on you.
  • You feel the tension between your mission and your role as a parent/partner/human, and you're ready to design a life that honours both.
  • If you feel that pull in your chest as you read this, that is your sign.
  • You're tired of the gap between the life you teach and the life you live.
  • You know you're capable of so much more, but you need clarity, structure and support to unlock it.
